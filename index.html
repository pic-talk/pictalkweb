We're trying to develop open source products for visually impaired persons. Also, everyone can involve this project as a volunteer. We will become a big community to bright the world.

Who We Are?

We are an open source team who try to help visually impaired persons in their social and educational lifes that located in Istanbul Turkey.

Connect With PIC-TALK

We are trying to develop different type of technologies. Also, we're combining them in a roof. Our technologies:

Smart Wristband : For sensing colors and feeling any image from computer or mobile platforms.--

Haptic Display : For sensing any shape of anything you want.

PIC-TALK Program : You can do a lot thing with this app. Learning information about images, reading books, drawing pictures etc.

PIC-TALK Website : Interracting with all systems and developers can contribute to the community with web-site.
